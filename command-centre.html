◈ Welcome, Operator
Here's how
it works

Four things to know before your first drill.

▸
Drills
Your core activity. Each drill is 10 questions from your chosen cert. Answer them, get XP, and watch your domain scores update in real time.
⬡
Daily Orders
Every day you get a mission: revisit questions you got wrong, drill your weakest domain, then reinforce a strong one. Complete all three to keep your streak alive.
◎
Readiness Map
Every domain in your cert is tracked separately. Hit 85% in a domain and it's marked mastered. Your goal is to get every domain to green before exam day.
★
Rank & XP
Correct answers earn XP. XP unlocks ranks from Recruit all the way up to Commander. Your rank reflects your total effort across all certs.
