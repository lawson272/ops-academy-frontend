The System

The C.O.M.M.A.N.D. Method

A seven-stage framework designed around the science of active recall and deliberate practice.

C
Calibrate
Diagnose your baseline across all domains before your first session. No guesswork — just a clear starting point.
O
Optimise
The AI targets your weakest domains first. No time wasted on what you already know.
M
Measure
XP, streaks and readiness scores updated live after every drill session.
M
Master
Repeat until performance is automatic, not just memorised. True mastery leaves no gaps.
A
Adapt
The engine evolves with you. As your scores rise, difficulty scales to keep you in the optimal learning zone.
N
Navigate
Know exactly where you stand — domain by domain — at any point before exam day.
D
Deliver
Walk into the exam ready. Not hopeful — ready. Your readiness score tells you before you sit.
